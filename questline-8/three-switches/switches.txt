Task:

Devise a strategy to determine which of the three switches controls a bulb in another room, entering the room only once.

Let's name the switches as 1,2,3.

Action 1
Turn on switch 1 and off it after a few minutes

Action2:
Turn on switch 2 and keep it turned on

Action 3:
Do not turn on switch 3.

Action 4:
Enter into the room which has the  bulb

if the bulb is hot when touched : It's switch 1.
if the bulb is glowing : It's switch 2
if the bulb is off : It's switch 3
